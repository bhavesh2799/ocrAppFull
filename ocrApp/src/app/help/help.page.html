ocrApp Guide
1. ocrApp allows only three languages at a time to keep the app performance optimal. 2. ocrApp is made only for printed text. 3. Handwritten text will not generate satisfiable outputs as ocrApp is trained on printed text data. 4. ocrApp app loads in English language by default. 5. Try keeping image size small by cropping the captured image. 6. Avoid bringing background noises to the captured text image. 7. If your task requires multiple languages, you can restart the app after the limit exceeds.
